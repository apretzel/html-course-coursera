Understanding the Parts of a Violin

The violin is a string instrument that is played with a bow. It has four strings and is known for its high-pitched sound.

main parts of the violin include the:

  • Body
  • Neck
  • Strings
  • Bridge
  • Tailpiece

Each part plays a crucial role in producing the instrument's unique sound and tone.
